The Maersk Oil Image Database provides you with a range of images for you to download for various types of media (web, newspaper print, magazines).

You can access the Maersk Oil Image Database from the Internet. All you need is your Login and Password which has been provided to you or you can obtain it by using the Contact feature.

The image database contains two paths: Images and Logos.

In Images you’ll find photos and graphics. In Logos you’ll find our company logos. In each of the two paths, you’ll find a number of main categories, which each has a scroll down function to sub-categories. The sub-categories lead you to an amount of images or graphics (number is listed next to sub-category in parenthesis) which you can select in order to begin your search.

All images are provided with the amount of data, which you’ll be able to see by choosing the image in question by clicking on it. Or, you can simply do a mouse-over on the thumbnail.

All images can be downloaded to your pc in various solutions “small” (for web), “medium” (for newspaper print), “large” (for magazines) and “original”.
